05 — FAQ

Questions.

Do I need to know anything about websites? +
Not at all. You tell us about your business — hours, services, photos. We handle everything. You don't write a line of code or touch any settings.
How long does it actually take? +
Most sites are live within 5–7 days. You'll get a preview before it goes live so you can ask for changes. We don't launch anything you haven't approved.
What do I need to give you? +
Business name, address, phone, hours, services, and any photos. Even iPhone shots from your Instagram work. We write all the text — you just review it.
Is there a monthly fee? +
No required monthly fee. You pay once and own the site. The only ongoing cost is your domain (~$12/year) if you want a custom URL — totally optional but recommended. The $30/month update plan is also optional.
Will I show up on Google? +
Yes. We set up your Google Business Profile as part of every Standard and Premium build — that's what gets you on Google Maps and local search results. You can also add SEO setup as a $30 add-on to any plan.
Can you add online booking? +
Yes — Square booking and payments is included in the Premium plan, or available as a $50 add-on to Starter or Standard. Clients book and pay directly from your site. You just need a free Square account.
How do I pay? +
We collect 50% upfront via Zelle to get started, and the remaining 50% once you've approved the preview. Simple, no contracts.
